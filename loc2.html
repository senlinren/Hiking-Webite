Sterling Rail Trail

Sterling is a city not too far from Leominster. As a matter of fact, it's about only a 10-15 min drive depending on traffic that day.
The Sterling Rail Trail is a higher traffic trail that you can find right next to downtown Sterling and next to Oh My Gosh Antiques.

Here are a few reasons on why I like this trail!

  1. It's all flat making it a non-intense walk
  2. The amount of wildlife here is more than you think. I've seen Bobcats!
  3. It's really not far from where I live making it an ideal driving distance.

Reasons why this trail may not be for you.

  • There are a lot of people that come to walk this trail.
  • Although there is a lot of nature, there are a lot of houses and buisnesses around too.
  • It could be too short of a trail for some.
